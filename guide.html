Life Topics Guide

Click a topic to receive biblical guidance, wisdom from Jesus, and practical steps for daily life.

Bible Verse:

“The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.”
— Psalm 34:18 (NIV)
Read full verse

Biblical Guidance:

God does not ignore your pain. Jesus Himself wept (John 11:35), showing that sadness is not weakness. When your heart feels heavy, God draws nearer, not farther.

How to Apply:

  • Talk honestly to God in prayer
  • Read Psalms when feeling overwhelmed
  • Ask God to replace sorrow with hope

Bible Verse:

“You make known to me the path of life; in your presence there is fullness of joy.”
— Psalm 16:11 (NIV)
Read full verse

Biblical Guidance:

True happiness does not come from possessions or success, but from walking with God. Jesus teaches that joy flows from obedience and relationship with Him.

How to Apply:

  • Spend daily time with God
  • Practice gratitude
  • Choose joy even during trials

Bible Verse:

“In your anger do not sin: Do not let the sun go down while you are still angry.”
— Ephesians 4:26 (NIV)
Read full verse

Biblical Guidance:

Anger itself is not sin, but uncontrolled anger leads to destruction. Jesus calls us to forgive quickly and seek peace.

How to Apply:

  • Pause before reacting
  • Pray for self-control
  • Choose forgiveness over resentment

Bible Verse:

“Anyone who looks at a woman lustfully has already committed adultery with her in his heart.”
— Matthew 5:28 (NIV)
Read full verse

Biblical Guidance:

Jesus teaches that purity begins in the heart. Lust objectifies others and pulls us away from God’s design for love.

How to Apply:

  • Guard your eyes and thoughts
  • Remove triggers that cause temptation
  • Ask God for a renewed heart

Bible Verse:

“So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God.”
— Isaiah 41:10 (NIV)
Read full verse

Biblical Guidance:

Fear fades when we remember who God is. Jesus constantly told His followers: “Do not be afraid.”

How to Apply:

  • Replace fear with prayer
  • Declare God’s promises aloud
  • Trust God’s presence in every situation
