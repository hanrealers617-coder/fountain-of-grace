Bible Verses

Here are some inspirational verses to guide your faith:

  • John 3:16 – God’s love for the world
  • Luke 14:26–27 – The cost of following Jesus
  • Matthew 5:16 – Shine your light before others
  • Philippians 4:13 – Strength through Christ

John 3:16 reminds us that God’s love is unconditional. Whoever believes in Him will have eternal life. This verse encourages us to trust Jesus and share His love with others.

Luke 14:26–27 teaches that following Jesus may require tough choices, putting Him above family, comfort, and self. Carrying your cross means fully committing to Christ’s path.

Tip: Memorize one verse per week and meditate on it daily.
